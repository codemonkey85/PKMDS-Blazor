When addressing pull request feedback:

1. **Only address unresolved feedback**: Focus on comments and suggestions that have not yet been addressed.
2. **Carefully review the feedback**: Make sure you understand the comments and suggestions provided by the reviewers.
3. **Break down the feedback into actionable tasks**: Identify the specific changes needed to address each comment.
4. **Update your code**: Make the necessary changes to your implementation based on the feedback.
5. **Test your changes**: Verify that your updates work as expected and do not introduce new issues.
6. **Respond to the feedback**: Leave comments on the pull request to indicate that you have addressed the feedback and provide any necessary explanations. Mark addressed comments as resolved to keep the review process organized.
